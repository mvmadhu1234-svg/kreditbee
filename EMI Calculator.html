What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI calculator is a useful financial tool that helps individuals calculate their Equated Monthly Instalments (EMIs) for loans such as personal loans, car loans, home loans, and more. By considering factors like loan amount, interest rate, and tenure, it helps calculate an estimated instalment amount that you can pay comfortably on a month-on-month basis

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is EMI in loan?
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Instalment. It’s the fixed amount you pay every month towards your loan, covering both principal and interest.
2. How do I calculate EMI manually?
3. Which is the best EMI calculator?
The best EMi calculator clearly shows a breakdown of principal vs interest, lets you adjust inputs, and provides visual charts for clarity.
4. Can I reduce my EMI?
Yes—you can either increase the loan tenure (which lowers EMI but increases total interest) or pre-pay, which reduces the total interest payable.
5. What happens if I miss an EMI?
Missing an EMI can lead to late payment fees, a negative impact on your credit score, and in severe cases, legal action.
6. What are the key benefits of using an EMI calculator for a personal loan?
  • Quickly estimate monthly payments based on amount, interest & tenure.
  • Helps with budgeting and loan comparison.
  • Shows clear breakdown of principal vs interest.
